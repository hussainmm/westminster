About Westminster

Westminster is the borough of the Houses of Parliment. It covers well known areas, such as, Oxford Street, Mayfair, Soho and Paddington.>

Famous landmarks includes, The London Eye, Houses of Parliment and Buckingham Palace.

A place of inclusivity and diversity. A place rich in history and culture, home to all different walks of life. Westminster is a top tourist attraction and a borough like no other.
